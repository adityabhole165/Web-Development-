Identifier Rules -

All Javascript variables must be identified with unique names(identifiers).

Names can contain letters, digits , underscores , and doller signs (no space)

Names must begin with a letters

Names can also begin with $ and _

Names are case sensitive(y and Y are different variables)

Reserved words (like Javascript keywords )CAN NOT be used as names
